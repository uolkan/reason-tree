ReasonTree

A small state-action tree for making Claude compare possible paths before committing to an answer.

Chess Demo

chess_mate2: ReasonTree selects Bxg5+.

Start position
1. Bxg5+
1... Kxg5
2. Qf4#
  1. Bxg5+: score=0.65, Sacrifices the bishop to force the black king onto g5 or f5.
  2. ...Kxg5: score=0.95, The king capture walks into Qf4 mate.
  3. Qf4#: score=1.00, The queen covers the escape squares and gives mate.
